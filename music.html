The Monkees music was hugely popular and widely known throughout the late 1960's and early 1970's The bands first single "Last Train to Clarksville", proved an immediate hit, shooting to #1 in the United States for a week. Over the next few years, the band produced 7 top 10 singles, 3 #1's and became a global smash hit with lively concerts and tours.
